Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

Credits are instantly added to your account when you join. Use them to book lessons, enter events, purchase gear, or unlock premium content. They're like cash within the platform.

Do credits expire?

Credits remain active as long as you maintain your membership. If you cancel, you have 90 days to use remaining credits.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes! Upgrade anytime and receive pro-rated credits immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next renewal date.

What if I cancel?

Cancel anytime with no penalties. Your membership continues until the end of your billing period, and you keep all earned credits for 90 days.

Is this a subscription?

Yes, memberships renew annually. You'll receive a full credit refresh with each renewal. You can turn off auto-renewal in your settings.

Can I gift a membership?

Absolutely! Contact our support team to purchase a gift membership for a fellow golfer.
