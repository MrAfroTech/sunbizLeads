Integration & Partnership Overview

POS and ticketing integrations, flexible workflows, onboarding timelines, and pricing—everything you need to know about partnering with Seamlessly.

1. POS & Ticketing Integrations

We integrate with Square, Clover, Lightspeed, Aloha, Shopify Pro, and Stripe (Toast pending). If you use a POS we haven’t partnered with yet, a simple request from you will expedite the integration.

We’ve integrated with Eventbrite and are in talks with Ticketmaster. If we move forward, we can easily integrate with your current ticketing system. We also offer our own ticketing system, Eventbrella. As with POS, a simple request will expedite integration with your preferred ticket platform.

All integrations are real-time API.

2. Workflow & Ordering Options

  • We work with your preferred workflow and can adjust as needed. Service staff can be expeditors only or full service—your setup, your choice.
  • We’re an add-on to your current system, or we can work together on a custom solution.
  • QR code ordering to avoid the friction of downloading an app—or use our app for a better experience for guests and your organization.
  • With the app: push notifications that work as marketing tools inside and outside the venue, plus data collection for deeper knowledge of consumer likes and preferences and better inventory management.

3. Hardware & Notifications

We integrate with your current POS; notifications are sent to your existing hardware and software. If you need a POS system, we can work with you to find hardware solutions that fit your exact needs.

4. Onboarding

  • As little as 72 hours when we only need your permission to integrate with your POS.
  • Two to three weeks if we need to build or configure your POS system.
  • Staff training is minimal—our core offering is mobile-first and designed to work with your current system.

5. Contracts

Our contracts are month to month. If hardware purchases are required, the hardware may have a cancellation fee or an annual contract until the hardware is paid in full, depending on the number of units you’d return.

6. Pricing

  • SaaS fee that can be waived once a certain number of transactions are fulfilled.
  • Fee depends on the number of features you want and any custom features requested.
  • We offer a pilot program for businesses where we can explore a mutually beneficial relationship.

7. Custom Features & Fan Engagement

We often brainstorm new features with partners. Our goal is to optimize fan engagement, and we have the marketing and tech skills to deliver new features with minimum onboarding time.
