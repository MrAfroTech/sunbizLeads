At this point in the sequence you've heard me talk about friction, about leaks, about the framework. Fair — you might be thinking: "This all sounds right. But what does it actually look like in a real venue?"

Let me show you.

The Orlando Pirates are a minor league baseball organization in Central Florida. When we started working with them, they had the same profile as most venues at their level: decent attendance, okay concession revenue, no unified view of their guest data, and a post-game survey that told them fans had a good time but not much else.

We didn't redesign the stadium. We didn't replace their POS. We mapped the guest journey, identified the three highest-friction moments, and fixed them in sequence.

The 31% per-cap lift wasn't from a new concession menu or a pricing change. It came from guests being able to get to a point of sale faster and making a second purchase because the halftime line was short enough to try. That's the compounding effect working in your favor instead of against you.

The 19% return rate improvement came from fixing the first-visit experience. Not from a loyalty app. From making the first night feel good enough that they wanted to come back on their own.

The Three Changes That Drove The Numbers
Before vs. After — In Concrete Terms
❌ Before

Entry scanners positioned in a single funnel. One line. One scanner operator. 9+ minute average wait at gate open.

✓ After

Distributed scan points with staggered staffing matched to arrival curve data. Average entry time dropped to 3.4 minutes.

❌ Before

Concession layout driven by physical convenience, not guest flow. Two stands near the main entrance, nothing near sections 200–220 where 38% of guests sat.

✓ After

Mobile stand positioned in the high-density section with a 5-item express menu. Per-cap in that section went from $4.20 to $9.80.

❌ Before

Guest data in three places: ticketing system, POS, and a spreadsheet from group sales. No one pulling it together. No post-visit communication for single-game buyers.

✓ After

Unified guest record. Automated post-game message within 90 minutes of the final out. Next-game offer delivered at the moment of highest return intent. Conversion rate: 22%.
